Aladdin's Gyro-Cery

Aladdin Gyro-Cery & Deli, 4139 University Wy NE, Seattle, WA 98105

Review by: notkyraxo

One of the best gyro sandwiches in the U-District! The staff is really friendly and they're always open late, which is a plus. The mix of beef and lamb is really superior. Plus it's pretty affordable!
